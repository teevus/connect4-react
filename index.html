Connect 4


Instructions

  • Players take turns dropping pieces into the board.
  • The piece will fall vertically down the board as far as it can go before stacking on top of another piece or reaching the bottom of the board.
  • The goal is to get 4 pieces in a row, either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.
  • Click on the down arrow, or an empty slot to drop a piece into that column

Source control available on Github: connect4-react
